# Product Availability Policy

**Document ID:** POL-010
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Stock Status

### 1.1 In Stock
- Available for immediate shipment
- Ships within 1-2 business days
- Quantity available shown
- Real-time inventory updates

### 1.2 Low Stock
- Limited quantity remaining
- May sell out before order placed
- First come, first served
- Consider purchasing quickly

### 1.3 Out of Stock
- Currently unavailable
- Option to sign up for restock alerts
- Estimated restock date (if available)
- Similar items suggested

### 1.4 Discontinued
- No longer carried
- No restock planned
- Remaining stock sold as-is
- Alternative products recommended

## 2. Inventory Updates

### 2.1 Real-Time Inventory
- Inventory updated every 15 minutes
- May sell out between updates
- High-demand items update more frequently
- Cart reservation for 15 minutes

### 2.2 Cart Reservation
- Items reserved for 15 minutes in cart
- Timer displayed at checkout
- Release if not purchased
- No guarantee after timer expires

### 2.3 Multiple Locations
- Inventory from multiple warehouses
- Closest warehouse selected automatically
- May ship from different locations
- Doesn't affect delivery time
