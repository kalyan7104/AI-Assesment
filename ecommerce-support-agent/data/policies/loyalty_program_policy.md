# Loyalty Program Policy

**Document ID:** POL-008
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Program Overview

### 1.1 Rewards Program
Our free loyalty program offers:
- Points on every purchase
- Exclusive discounts
- Early access to sales
- Birthday rewards
- Free shipping benefits

### 1.2 Membership Tiers
- **Silver**: 0-999 points (automatic upon signup)
- **Gold**: 1,000-4,999 points
- **Platinum**: 5,000-9,999 points
- **Diamond**: 10,000+ points

### 1.3 Enrollment
- Free to join
- Sign up online or in-store
- Instant enrollment
- Start earning immediately

## 2. Earning Points

### 2.1 Purchase Points
- Earn 1 point per $1 spent
- Points on pre-tax amount
- Excludes shipping and fees
- Posted within 24 hours of delivery

### 2.2 Bonus Point Opportunities
- Account creation: 100 bonus points
- First purchase: 200 bonus points
- Product reviews: 25 points per review
- Referrals: 500 points per successful referral
- Birthday month: Double points
- Social media follows: 50 points each

### 2.3 Special Promotions
- Double point days
- Category-specific bonuses
- Seasonal promotions
- Email exclusive offers

### 2.4 Points Not Earned On
- Taxes and fees
- Shipping costs
- Gift card purchases
- Previous purchases (before enrollment)
- Returned items

## 3. Redeeming Points

### 3.1 Redemption Values
- 100 points = $1 off
- Minimum redemption: 500 points ($5)
- Maximum per order: 50% of order value
- No cash value

### 3.2 How to Redeem
- At checkout: Select points to use
- Automatic calculation of discount
- Applied before tax
- Cannot be reversed after order placed

### 3.3 Redemption Restrictions
- Cannot redeem on gift cards
- Cannot combine with some promotions
- One redemption per order
- Points deducted immediately

## 4. Tier Benefits

### 4.1 Silver Tier (0-999 points)
- Earn 1 point per $1

### 4.2 Gold Tier (1,000-4,999 points)

### 4.4 Diamond Tier (10,000+ points)
All Platinum benefits plus:

- Annual $100 reward

## 5. Points Expiration

### 5.1 Expiration Policy
- Points expire after 12 months of inactivity
- Activity = purchase or points redemption
- Warning email sent 30 days before expiration
- Expired points cannot be reinstated

### 5.2 Keeping Points Active
To prevent expiration:
- Make a purchase
- Redeem points
- Earn points through activities
- Any account activity resets 12-month clock

### 5.3 Account Closure
If account closed:
- All points forfeited
- Cannot be transferred
- Cannot be redeemed after closure
- 30-day grace period to reactivate

## 6. Tier Qualification

### 6.1 Tier Advancement
- Based on points earned in 12-month period
- Automatic tier upgrade
- Benefits activate immediately
- Email notification sent

### 6.2 Tier Maintenance
- Maintain tier with annual point threshold
- Rolling 12-month calculation
- Tier reviewed monthly
- Downgrade if threshold not met

### 6.3 Tier Downgrade
- Warning email 60 days before downgrade
- Grace period to earn needed points
- Automatic downgrade if threshold not met
- Can re-qualify anytime

- Fraudulent purchases
