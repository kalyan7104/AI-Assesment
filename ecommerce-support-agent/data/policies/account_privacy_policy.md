# Account and Privacy Policy

**Document ID:** POL-003
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Account Creation

### 1.1 Registration Requirements
- Valid email address required
- Must be 18+ years old or have parental consent
- Accurate information required
- One account per person

### 1.2 Account Verification
- Email verification required within 24 hours
- Phone verification for orders over $500
- Additional verification for high-risk transactions

### 1.3 Guest Checkout
- Available for all purchases
- Order tracking via email link
- Can convert to account post-purchase

## 2. Account Security

### 2.1 Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, and number
- Special characters recommended
- Cannot reuse last 5 passwords

### 2.2 Two-Factor Authentication
- Optional but recommended
- SMS or authenticator app
- Required for accounts with saved payment methods
- Required for orders over $1,000

### 2.3 Suspicious Activity
Account locked if we detect:
- Multiple failed login attempts (5+)
- Login from unusual location
- Unusual purchasing patterns
- Potential account takeover

### 2.4 Account Recovery
- Password reset via email
- Security questions as backup
- Customer service verification for locked accounts
- 24-hour cooldown for security changes

## 3. Personal Information Collection

### 3.1 Information We Collect
- Name, email, phone number
- Billing and shipping addresses
- Payment information (encrypted)
- Purchase history
- Browsing behavior and preferences
- Device and browser information
- IP address and location data

### 3.2 How We Use Your Information
- Process and fulfill orders
- Communicate about orders and account
- Personalize shopping experience
- Send marketing communications (opt-in)
- Prevent fraud and improve security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Improve our services

### 3.3 Information We Don't Sell
We never sell your personal information to third parties.

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 Service Providers
We share data with:
- Payment processors (encrypted)
- Shipping carriers (address only)
- Email service providers
- Analytics providers
- Customer service platforms

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when:
- Required by law or court order
- Protecting our rights or property
- Investigating fraud or security issues
- Responding to government requests

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, customer data may be transferred to new entity.

## 5. Marketing Communications

### 5.1 Email Marketing
- Promotional emails (opt-in required)

### 5.2 Opt-Out Options
- Unsubscribe link in every email

### 5.3 Transactional Emails
Cannot opt-out of:

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

### 6.1 Cookie Types
- Essential cookies (required for site function)
- Performance cookies (analytics)
- Functional cookies (preferences)
- Advertising cookies (targeted ads)

### 6.2 Cookie Management
- Cookie consent banner on first visit
- Manage preferences in account settings
- Browser settings to block cookies
- Some features may not work without cookies

### 6.3 Third-Party Tracking
- Google Analytics
- Facebook Pixel
- Advertising networks
- Social media plugins

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Active Accounts
Data retained while account is active plus:
- Purchase history: 7 years (tax/legal requirements)
- Payment information: Until card expires or removed
- Browsing data: 2 years
- Marketing preferences: Until opt-out

### 7.2 Inactive Accounts
Accounts inactive for 3+ years:
- Warning email sent
- 30 days to reactivate
- Account and data deleted after 30 days
- Purchase history retained for legal compliance

### 7.3 Deleted Accounts
Upon account deletion:
- Personal information removed within 30 days
- Purchase history anonymized
- Legal/financial records retained per regulations
- Backup copies deleted within 90 days

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

### 8.1 Access Rights
You can request:
- Copy of all personal data we hold
- Information about how data is used
- List of third parties with access
- Response within 30 days

### 8.2 Correction Rights
You can:
- Update account information anytime
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Add missing information

### 8.3 Deletion Rights
You can request:
- Account deletion
- Removal of specific data
- Exceptions for legal/financial records

### 8.4 Portability Rights
You can request:
- Data in machine-readable format
- Transfer to another service
- Provided within 30 days

### 8.5 Objection Rights
You can object to:
- Marketing communications
- Automated decision-making
- Profiling for marketing purposes

## 9. Regional Privacy Laws

### 9.1 GDPR (European Union)
EU customers have additional rights:
- Right to be forgotten
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to lodge complaint with supervisory authority

### 9.2 CCPA (California)
California residents can:
- Know what data is collected
- Know if data is sold or shared
- Opt-out of data sale
- Request deletion
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights

### 9.3 Other US State Laws
Compliance with privacy laws in:
- Virginia (VCDPA)
- Colorado (CPA)
- Connecticut (CTDPA)
- Utah (UCPA)
