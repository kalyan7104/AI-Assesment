# Payment Methods Policy

**Document ID:** POL-004
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Accepted Payment Methods

### 1.1 Credit Cards
Accepted cards:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

### 1.2 Debit Cards
- All major debit cards with Visa/Mastercard logo
- PIN not required for online purchases
- Processed as credit transaction

### 1.3 Digital Wallets
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Shop Pay
- Amazon Pay

### 1.4 Buy Now, Pay Later
- Affirm (orders $50-$17,500)
- Klarna (orders $35-$10,000)
- Afterpay (orders $35-$1,000)
- PayPal Pay in 4

### 1.5 Gift Cards and Store Credit
- Our branded gift cards
- Store credit from returns
- Promotional credits
- Cannot be combined with some offers

### 1.6 Bank Transfer (ACH)
- Available for orders over $500
- 3-5 business day processing
- Order ships after payment clears
- $2.50 processing fee

## 2. Payment Security

### 2.1 Encryption
- 256-bit SSL encryption
- PCI DSS Level 1 compliant
- Tokenization for stored cards
- No card numbers stored on our servers

### 2.2 Fraud Prevention
- Address Verification System (AVS)
- CVV verification required
- 3D Secure for international cards
- Automated fraud detection

### 2.3 Secure Checkout
- HTTPS on all pages
- Security badges displayed
- No payment info in emails
- Session timeout after 15 minutes

## 3. Payment Authorization

### 3.1 Authorization Hold
- Card authorized at time of order
- Hold remains until shipment
- Actual charge when item ships
- Authorization expires after 7 days

### 3.2 Split Shipments
- Charged separately for each shipment
- Original authorization may be adjusted
- Notification sent for each charge

### 3.3 Pre-Orders
- Card authorized at order time
- Charged when item ships
- If card expires, notification sent to update

## 4. Payment Declined

### 4.1 Common Reasons
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect card information
- Expired card
- Billing address mismatch
- Card issuer decline
- Fraud prevention trigger

### 4.2 Resolution Steps
1. Verify card information
2. Contact card issuer
3. Try alternative payment method
4. Contact our customer service

### 4.3 Retry Policy
- 2 automatic retry attempts
- 24 hours between attempts
- Order cancelled after 3 failures
- Customer notified of each attempt

## 5. Saved Payment Methods

### 5.1 Storing Cards
- Save cards for faster checkout
- Encrypted and tokenized
- Can save multiple cards
- Set default payment method

### 5.2 Managing Saved Cards
- Add, edit, or remove anytime
- Update expiration dates
- Change billing address
- Requires password confirmation

### 5.3 Security Requirements
- Two-factor authentication recommended
- Password required to view full card number
- Last 4 digits displayed only
- Automatic removal of expired cards

## 6. International Payments

### 6.1 Currency Conversion
- Prices displayed in USD
- Card issuer handles conversion
- Exchange rate set by card issuer
- Foreign transaction fees may apply

### 6.2 International Cards
- Most international cards accepted
- 3D Secure verification required
- Additional verification may be needed
- Some countries restricted due to fraud

### 6.3 Supported Countries
We accept payments from 100+ countries. Restricted countries:
- Countries under US sanctions
- High-fraud risk regions
- See website for complete list

## 7. Buy Now, Pay Later Details

### 7.1 Affirm
- Split into 3, 6, or 12 monthly payments
- 0-30% APR based on credit check
- Soft credit check (doesn't affect score)
- Instant approval decision
- First payment due 30 days after purchase

### 7.2 Klarna
- Pay in 4 interest-free payments
- Or pay in 30 days
- No credit check for Pay in 4
- Automatic payments every 2 weeks

### 7.3 Afterpay
- 4 interest-free payments
- Every 2 weeks
- First payment at purchase
- No credit check
- Late fees apply for missed payments

### 7.4 PayPal Pay in 4
- 4 interest-free payments
- Every 2 weeks
- No late fees
- Integrated with PayPal account

### 7.5 BNPL Terms
- Customer agreement with BNPL provider
- We're not responsible for payment issues
- Returns processed per our policy
- Refunds sent to BNPL provider

## 8. Gift Cards

### 8.1 Purchasing Gift Cards
- Available in $10-$500 denominations
- Digital or physical cards
- No expiration date
- No fees

### 8.2 Using Gift Cards
- Enter code at checkout
- Check balance online or by phone
- Combine multiple gift cards
- Combine with other payment methods

### 8.3 Gift Card Restrictions
- Cannot purchase other gift cards
- Cannot be redeemed for cash (except where required by law)
- Non-refundable
- Not replaceable if lost or stolen (unless registered)

### 8.4 Gift Card Registration
- Register card to protect balance
- Replacement available if lost
- Transfer balance to new card
- Track purchase history

## 9. Store Credit

### 9.1 Receiving Store Credit
Issued for:
- Returns (when chosen over refund)
- Promotional bonuses
- Service recovery
- Loyalty rewards

### 9.2 Using Store Credit
- Automatically applied at checkout
- No expiration date
- Transferable to others
- Cannot be redeemed for cash

### 9.3 Store Credit Balance
- View in account dashboard
- Email notifications when issued
- Transaction history available
- Combine with other payment methods

## 10. Payment Disputes

### 10.1 Billing Errors
If you notice incorrect charge:
- Contact us within 60 days
- Provide order number and details
- Investigation within 5 business days
- Resolution within 10 business days

### 10.2 Chargeback Policy
Before filing chargeback:
- Contact our customer service first
- Most issues resolved quickly
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension
- Chargeback fees may apply

### 10.3 Dispute Resolution
For payment disputes:
- Email: billing@ecommerce-example.com
- Phone: 1-800-BILLING (1-800-245-5464)
- Provide documentation
- Resolution within 10 business days

## 11. Refunds

### 11.1 Refund Timeline
- Processed within 5-7 business days
- Refund to original payment method
- Bank processing: additional 3-5 days
- Notification sent when processed

### 11.2 Refund Methods
- Credit/debit cards: Original card
- PayPal: PayPal account
- BNPL: Through BNPL provider
- Gift cards: Store credit only
- Bank transfer: Original account

### 11.3 Partial Refunds
- Prorated for used services
- Restocking fees deducted
- Shipping costs excluded (unless our error)
- Promotional discounts adjusted

## 12. Promotional Codes and Discounts

### 12.1 Applying Codes
- Enter at checkout
- One code per order (unless stated)
- Cannot be combined with some sales
- Case-sensitive codes

### 12.2 Code Restrictions
- Expiration dates apply
- Minimum purchase requirements
- Excluded items/categories
- One-time use unless stated

### 12.3 Code Issues
If code doesn't work:
- Check expiration date
- Verify minimum purchase met
- Ensure items are eligible
- Contact support for assistance

## 13. Pricing and Taxes

### 13.1 Price Display
- Prices in USD
- Taxes calculated at checkout
- Shipping costs added at checkout
- Final total shown before payment

### 13.2 Sales Tax
Collected for:
- Orders shipped to states where we have nexus
- Based on shipping address
- Rates set by state/local government
- Included in order total

### 13.3 Price Changes
- Prices subject to change
- Price at time of order honored
- No price adjustments after purchase
- Exception: Price match policy (see POL-009)

### 13.4 Price Errors
If obvious pricing error:
- We reserve right to cancel order
- Customer notified immediately
- Full refund issued
- Option to purchase at correct price

## 14. Subscription Payments

### 14.1 Auto-Renewal
For subscription services:
- Automatic billing on renewal date
- Notification 7 days before charge
- Update payment method anytime
- Cancel before renewal to avoid charge

### 14.2 Failed Subscription Payments
- 3 retry attempts over 10 days
- Email notification for each failure
- Service suspended after 3 failures
- Reactivate by updating payment

### 14.3 Subscription Refunds
- Pro-rated refunds for cancellations
- No refunds for partial months
- Annual subscriptions: refund unused months
- Processing time: 5-7 business days

## 15. Payment Verification

### 15.1 Additional Verification Required
May be required for:
- First-time customers
- Orders over $500
- Shipping to different address than billing
- High-risk transactions

### 15.2 Verification Methods
- Photo ID
- Credit card statement
- Billing address confirmation
- Phone verification

### 15.3 Verification Timeline
- Request sent via email
- 48 hours to provide documents
- Order held until verified
- Cancelled if not verified in time
