# Gift Cards Policy

## 1. Purchasing Gift Cards

### 1.1 Available Denominations
- Physical cards: $10, $25, $50, $100, $200, $500
- Digital cards: Any amount from $5 to $500
- Custom amounts available for digital cards

### 1.2 Purchase Methods
- Online: Website or mobile app
- In-store: At any retail location
- Phone: Call customer service
- Bulk orders: Contact corporate sales

### 1.3 Payment Methods
Gift cards can be purchased with:
- Credit/debit cards
- PayPal
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Bank transfer (bulk orders)
- Cannot purchase with other gift cards

## 7. Restrictions and Exclusions

### 7.1 Cannot Purchase
Gift cards cannot buy:
- Other gift cards
- Lottery tickets
- Money orders
- Cash equivalents

### 7.2 Cannot Redeem For
- Cash (except where required by law)
- Credit to credit card
- Check or money order
- Wire transfer

### 7.3 Excluded Items
Some items may be excluded:
- Third-party marketplace items (seller-specific)
- Special order items (case-by-case)
- Services (case-by-case)

## 8. Lost or Stolen Cards

### 8.1 Registered Cards
If registered card is lost/stolen:
- Contact customer service immediately
- Card will be deactivated
- Balance transferred to new card
- New card issued within 5-7 days
- Digital replacement available instantly

### 8.2 Unregistered Cards
If unregistered card is lost/stolen:
- Cannot be replaced
- Balance cannot be recovered
- Treat like cash
- Register cards to protect balance

### 8.3 Fraudulent Use
If card used fraudulently:
- Report immediately
- Investigation within 5 business days
- Balance restored if fraud confirmed
- New card issued

## 9. Damaged Cards

### 9.1 Physical Card Damage
If card is damaged:
- Contact customer service
- Provide card number if readable
- Provide proof of purchase
- Replacement issued within 5-7 days

### 9.2 Unreadable Cards
If code is unreadable:
- Photo of card required
- Proof of purchase required
- Original receipt helpful
- Manual verification process

## 15. Gift Card Scams

### 15.1 Common Scams
Beware of scams requesting gift cards for:
- Tax payments
- Utility bills
- Government fees
- Bail money
- Debt collection
- Prize claims

### 15.2 Legitimate Uses
We never ask for gift cards for:
- Payment of bills or fees
- Verification purposes
- Account security
- Prize claims

### 15.3 If Scammed
If you were scammed:
- Contact us immediately
- File police report
- Provide transaction details
- We'll assist investigation
- Cannot guarantee recovery

### 15.4 Prevention Tips
- Never share card codes via phone/email
- Only buy from authorized retailers
- Verify recipient identity
- Be suspicious of urgent requests
- Government agencies don't accept gift cards
