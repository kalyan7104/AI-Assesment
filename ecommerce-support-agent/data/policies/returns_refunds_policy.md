# Returns and Refunds Policy

**Document ID:** POL-001
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 2.1

## 1. General Return Policy

### 1.1 Standard Return Window
Customers may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging intact.

### 1.2 Extended Return Window
During holiday season (November 1 - December 31), return window extends to 60 days from delivery date.

### 1.3 Return Eligibility
To be eligible for return:
- Item must be unused and in original condition
- Original packaging must be intact
- All tags and labels must be attached
- Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) required

## 2. Refund Processing

### 2.1 Refund Timeline
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after receiving returned item
- Original payment method will be credited
- Bank processing may take additional 3-5 business days

### 2.2 Refund Amount
- Full purchase price refunded for eligible returns
- Original shipping costs non-refundable unless item was defective or incorrect
- Return shipping costs are customer's responsibility unless error on our part

### 2.3 Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued for:
- Items with obvious signs of use
- Items missing original packaging
- Items returned more than 30 days after delivery
- Items with missing accessories or documentation

## 3. Non-Returnable Items

### 3.1 Final Sale Items
The following items are FINAL SALE and cannot be returned:
- Items marked as "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Clearance items (50% off or more)
- Gift cards and promotional codes
- Digital downloads and software

### 3.2 Hygiene and Safety Items
For health and safety reasons, the following cannot be returned once opened:
- Personal care items (cosmetics, skincare, fragrances)
- Undergarments and swimwear
- Earrings and body jewelry
- Medical devices and health monitors

### 3.3 Perishable Items
- Food items, beverages, and supplements
- Fresh flowers and plants
- Items with expiration dates
- Temperature-sensitive products

**Exception:** Perishable items may be refunded if delivered damaged, spoiled, or past expiration date. Customer must provide photographic evidence within 24 hours of delivery.

### 3.4 Custom and Personalized Items
- Made-to-order items
- Engraved or monogrammed products
- Custom-sized items
- Personalized gifts

**Exception:** Custom items may be returned if they arrive damaged or if there was an error in customization on our part.

## 4. Defective or Damaged Items

### 4.1 Reporting Damage
Customers must report damaged or defective items within 48 hours of delivery by:
- Contacting customer support
- Providing order number
- Submitting clear photos of damage
- Describing the defect or damage

### 4.2 Resolution Options
For defective or damaged items, we offer:
- Full refund (including original shipping)
- Free replacement shipment
- Store credit with 10% bonus

### 4.3 No Return Required
For items under $25 that arrive damaged, customers may keep the item and receive full refund.

## 5. Wrong Item Received

### 5.1 Our Error
If we shipped the wrong item:
- Full refund including all shipping costs
- Free return shipping label provided
- Option for free replacement shipment
- 15% discount code for inconvenience

### 5.2 Verification Required
Customer must provide:
- Photo of item received
- Photo of shipping label
- Order confirmation number

## 6. Regional Variations

### 6.1 California Residents
Per California law, customers have right to return items within 7 days of purchase for store credit, even for final sale items (excluding perishables and hygiene items).

### 6.2 European Union
EU customers have 14-day cooling-off period for all items (excluding hygiene items and perishables) per EU Consumer Rights Directive.

### 6.3 International Returns
- International customers responsible for return shipping costs
- Customs fees and duties non-refundable
- Extended processing time (10-15 business days)

## 7. Marketplace Seller Items

### 7.1 Third-Party Seller Returns
Items sold by marketplace sellers follow seller's individual return policy:
- Return window may vary (typically 14-30 days)
- Seller's policy displayed on product page
- Returns processed directly with seller
- Platform may mediate disputes

### 7.2 Platform Guarantee
If marketplace seller doesn't respond within 48 hours or refuses valid return, platform will:
- Issue refund from seller's account
- Handle return logistics
- Apply seller penalty

## 8. Restocking Fees

### 8.1 Standard Items
No restocking fee for most returns.

### 8.2 Large Items
15% restocking fee applies to:
- Furniture over 50 lbs
- Large appliances
- Electronics over $500
- Items requiring special handling

### 8.3 Fee Waiver
Restocking fee waived if:
- Item is defective
- Wrong item was shipped
- Item significantly not as described

## 9. Return Shipping

### 9.1 Customer Responsibility
Customer pays return shipping unless:
- Item is defective or damaged
- Wrong item was shipped
- Item not as described

### 9.2 Prepaid Labels
We provide prepaid return labels for:
- Defective items
- Our shipping errors
- Premium members (free returns benefit)

### 9.3 Return Shipping Costs
Deducted from refund amount if customer is responsible:
- Standard items: $6.99
- Large items: $15.99 - $49.99
- International: Actual carrier cost

## 10. Refund to Original Payment Method

### 10.1 Credit/Debit Cards
Refund credited to original card within 5-7 business days.

### 10.2 PayPal
Refund to PayPal account within 3-5 business days.

### 10.3 Gift Cards
Purchases made with gift cards refunded as store credit.

### 10.4 Buy Now Pay Later
Refunds for BNPL purchases (Affirm, Klarna, etc.) processed through payment provider. Customer remains responsible for any interest or fees per provider's terms.

## 11. Store Credit Option

### 11.1 Store Credit Benefits
Customers may choose store credit instead of refund:
- Immediate credit (no processing wait)
- 10% bonus credit added
- No expiration date
- Transferable to others

### 11.2 Store Credit Restrictions
- Cannot be redeemed for cash
- Cannot be used for gift card purchases
- Valid for online and in-store purchases

## 12. Return Fraud Prevention

### 12.1 Return Limits
Accounts with excessive returns (>40% of orders) may be:
- Flagged for review
- Required to provide additional documentation
- Restricted from future purchases

### 12.2 Serial Number Tracking
Electronics and high-value items tracked by serial number to prevent return fraud.
