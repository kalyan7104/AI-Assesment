# Product Warranty Policy

**Document ID:** POL-005
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Standard Warranty Coverage

### 1.1 Warranty Period
- Electronics: 1 year from purchase date
- Appliances: 1 year from purchase date
- Furniture: 90 days from delivery
- Clothing/Accessories: 30 days from purchase
- Other items: 30 days from purchase

### 1.2 What's Covered
- Manufacturing defects
- Material defects
- Workmanship issues
- Functional failures under normal use

### 1.3 What's Not Covered
- Normal wear and tear
- Accidental damage
- Misuse or abuse
- Unauthorized repairs
- Cosmetic damage not affecting function
- Damage from improper storage
- Acts of nature (fire, flood, lightning)

## 2. Manufacturer's Warranty

### 2.1 Manufacturer Coverage
Many products include manufacturer's warranty:
- Terms set by manufacturer
- Duration varies by product
- Registration may be required
- Proof of purchase required

### 2.2 Our Role
We facilitate manufacturer warranty claims:
- Provide purchase documentation
- Connect customer with manufacturer
- Assist with claim process
- Not responsible for manufacturer decisions

### 2.3 Manufacturer Contact
- Warranty information included with product
- Contact details on product packaging
- Manufacturer website for registration
- We can provide contact information

## 3. Extended Warranty

### 3.1 Extended Protection Plans
Available for purchase:
- Electronics: 2 or 3 year plans
- Appliances: 2, 3, or 5 year plans
- Furniture: 2 or 3 year plans
- Coverage starts after manufacturer warranty ends

### 3.2 Extended Warranty Coverage
Includes:
- Mechanical/electrical failures
- Parts and labor
- No deductibles
- Replacement if unrepairable
- 24/7 claim support

### 3.3 Extended Warranty Cost
- 10-20% of product price
- One-time payment
- Must be purchased with product or within 30 days
- Non-refundable after 30 days

### 3.4 Extended Warranty Claims
- File claim online or by phone
- Diagnosis by authorized technician
- Repair or replacement within 7-10 days
- Shipping covered for repairs

## 4. Warranty Claims Process

### 4.1 Filing a Claim
To file warranty claim:
1. Contact customer service
2. Provide order number and purchase date
3. Describe the defect or issue
4. Provide photos/videos if requested
5. Receive claim number

### 4.2 Required Documentation
- Proof of purchase (order confirmation)
- Product serial number (if applicable)
- Photos of defect
- Description of issue
- Troubleshooting steps attempted

### 4.3 Claim Review
- Review within 2-3 business days
- May require additional information
- Approval or denial notification
- If approved, resolution options provided

### 4.4 Resolution Timeline
- Replacement: 5-7 business days
- Repair: 10-15 business days
- Refund: 5-7 business days
- Extended warranty claims: 7-10 business days

## 5. Warranty Resolution Options

### 5.1 Replacement
For defective items:
- New replacement shipped
- Same model or comparable substitute
- Free shipping both ways
- Return defective item within 14 days

### 5.2 Repair
For repairable items:
- Authorized service center repair
- Free shipping to/from repair center
- Loaner available for some items
- Repair completed within 15 business days

### 5.3 Refund
If replacement/repair not possible:
- Full refund of purchase price
- Includes original shipping (if defective)
- Processed within 5-7 business days
- Option for store credit with 10% bonus

### 5.4 Store Credit
Alternative to refund:
- 110% of purchase price in store credit
- No expiration date
- Immediate credit (no processing wait)
- Can be used for any purchase

## 6. Electronics Warranty

### 6.1 Coverage Details
Electronics covered for:
- Power failures
- Screen defects
- Button/control malfunctions
- Battery issues (if not user-replaceable)
- Software defects
- Connectivity issues

### 6.2 Not Covered
- Cracked screens (unless manufacturing defect)
- Water damage
- Lost or stolen items
- Software issues from user modifications
- Accessories (cables, cases, etc.)
- Cosmetic damage

### 6.3 Battery Warranty
- Covered if fails to hold charge
- Must be below 80% capacity within warranty period
- User-replaceable batteries not covered
- Battery life degradation is normal

## 7. Appliance Warranty

### 7.1 Major Appliances
Coverage includes:
- Refrigerators, washers, dryers
- Dishwashers, ovens, ranges
- Parts and labor for 1 year
- In-home service for large appliances

### 7.2 Small Appliances
Coverage includes:
- Coffee makers, blenders, toasters
- Vacuums, air purifiers
- Return to service center or replacement
- No in-home service

### 7.3 Installation Issues
- Installation defects covered
- Must be installed by authorized technician
- DIY installation voids warranty
- Improper installation not covered

## 8. Furniture Warranty

### 8.1 Structural Warranty
90-day coverage for:
- Frame defects
- Joint failures
- Structural integrity issues
- Manufacturing defects

### 8.2 Fabric/Upholstery
30-day coverage for:
- Seam failures
- Fabric defects
- Zipper malfunctions
- Color inconsistencies

### 8.3 Not Covered
- Stains or spills
- Pet damage
- Fading from sunlight
- Sagging from normal use
- Scratches or dents

## 9. Refurbished Products

### 9.1 Refurbished Warranty
- 90-day warranty on refurbished items
- Covers same defects as new items
- Clearly marked as refurbished
- Tested and certified before sale

### 9.2 Refurbished Limitations
- May have cosmetic imperfections
- Original accessories may not be included
- Original packaging not included
- No manufacturer warranty

### 9.3 Certified Refurbished
Premium refurbished items:
- 1-year warranty (same as new)
- Like-new condition
- Original accessories included
- Certified by manufacturer

### 10.1 Open Box Warranty

- Customer returns in excellent condition
- Fully functional and tested
- May have minor packaging damage

### 10.2 Open Box Discounts
- 10-30% off original price

## 11. Warranty Exclusions

### 11.1 Misuse and Abuse
Warranty void if:
- Used for commercial purposes (unless specified)
- Modified or altered
- Repaired by unauthorized service
- Used contrary to instructions
- Subjected to abnormal conditions

### 11.2 Unauthorized Repairs
- Repairs must be authorized by us
- Unauthorized repairs void warranty
- Contact us before seeking repair
- We'll direct to authorized service center

### 11.3 Missing Serial Numbers
- Removed or altered serial numbers void warranty
- Serial number required for claim
- Helps prevent fraud
- Protects all customers

## 12. International Warranty

### 12.1 US Purchases
- Warranty valid in US only
- International use may void warranty
- Voltage differences not covered
- Return shipping from international locations not covered

### 12.2 International Purchases
- Warranty valid in country of purchase
- May not be honored in other countries
- Contact local customer service
- Manufacturer warranty may differ by region

## 13. Warranty Transfer

### 13.1 Transferability
- Warranty transfers to new owner
- Proof of original purchase required
- Transfer must be documented
- Contact customer service to transfer

### 13.2 Gift Purchases
- Warranty valid for gift recipients
- Gift receipt serves as proof of purchase

- Registration recommended
