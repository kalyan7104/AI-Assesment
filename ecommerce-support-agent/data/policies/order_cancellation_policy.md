# Order Cancellation Policy

**Document ID:** POL-012
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Cancellation Overview

### 1.1 When You Can Cancel
- Before order ships: Full cancellation available
- After shipment: Must refuse delivery or return
- Custom items: Limited cancellation window
- Digital items: No cancellation after delivery

### 1.2 How to Cancel
- Online: Account dashboard
- Phone: 1-800-CANCEL-ORDER (1-800-226-2356)
- Live chat: Available 24/7
- Email: cancellations@ecommerce-example.com

### 1.3 Cancellation Confirmation
- Immediate confirmation email
- Refund timeline provided
- Order status updated
- Tracking cancelled

## 2. Cancellation Timeframes

### 2.1 Immediate Cancellation
Within 1 hour of order:
- Instant cancellation available
- Self-service through account
- No fees
- Full refund

### 2.2 Before Processing
1-24 hours after order:
- Contact customer service
- Usually can be cancelled
- No fees if successful
- Refund within 5-7 business days

### 2.3 During Processing
Order being prepared:
- May not be cancellable
- Attempt to stop processing
- If shipped, must return
- No cancellation fees

### 2.4 After Shipment
Once shipped:
- Cannot cancel
- Refuse delivery for full refund
- Or receive and return per return policy
- Return shipping may apply

## 3. Cancellation Methods

### 3.1 Self-Service Cancellation
Through account dashboard:
1. Log into account
2. Go to Order History
3. Select order
4. Click "Cancel Order"
5. Confirm cancellation
6. Receive confirmation email

### 3.2 Customer Service Cancellation
Contact support:
- Provide order number
- Verify account information
- Reason for cancellation (optional)
- Confirmation provided
- Refund timeline given

### 3.3 Automatic Cancellation
Orders auto-cancelled if:
- Payment fails after 3 attempts
- Fraud detected
- Item unavailable
- Address undeliverable
- Customer notified immediately

## 4. Refund Processing

### 4.1 Refund Timeline
- Cancellation processed: Immediate
- Refund initiated: Within 24 hours
- Refund to account: 5-7 business days
- Bank processing: Additional 3-5 days

### 4.2 Refund Method
- Original payment method
- Full order amount
- Shipping fees refunded (if paid)
- Promotional discounts preserved
- Gift cards refunded as store credit

### 4.3 Partial Cancellations
For multi-item orders:
- Cancel individual items
- Refund for cancelled items only
- Remaining items still ship
- Shipping fees prorated

## 5. Special Order Types

### 5.1 Pre-Orders
- Cancel anytime before shipment
- No fees
- Full refund
- Release date changes don't affect cancellation rights

### 5.2 Back Orders
- Cancel anytime before shipment
- No penalty
- Full refund
- Can switch to in-stock alternative

### 5.3 Custom/Personalized Items
- 24-hour cancellation window
- After 24 hours: Production may have started
- Cannot cancel once production begins
- Refund only if defective

### 5.4 Made-to-Order Items
- Cancel within 2 hours of order
- After 2 hours: 50% cancellation fee
- After production starts: No cancellation
- Exception: Our error or defect

## 6. Subscription Cancellations

### 6.1 Subscription Orders
- Cancel next shipment anytime
- Must cancel before processing date
- Processing date shown in account
- No fees for cancellation

### 6.2 Subscription Service
- Cancel subscription anytime
- No cancellation fees
- Prorated refund for annual plans
- Current period fulfilled

### 6.3 Auto-Renewal
- Cancel before renewal date
- No charge if cancelled in time
- Renewal date in account settings
- Reminder email sent 7 days before

## 7. Cancellation Fees

### 7.1 Standard Orders
- No cancellation fees
- Before shipment: Free cancellation
- After shipment: Return shipping may apply

### 7.2 Custom Orders
- Within 24 hours: No fee
- After 24 hours: Up to 50% fee
- After production: No cancellation
- Fees clearly stated at order time

### 7.3 Large/Freight Items
- Before shipment: No fee
- After shipment: Return freight charges apply
- Restocking fee: 15%
- White glove delivery: Non-refundable

### 7.4 International Orders
- Before shipment: No fee
- After shipment: Return shipping costs
- Customs fees non-refundable
- Duties non-refundable

## 8. Partial Cancellations

### 8.1 Multi-Item Orders
Can cancel:
- Individual items
- Specific quantities
- Entire order
- Before shipment only

### 8.2 Split Shipments
- Cancel items not yet shipped
- Cannot cancel shipped items
- Partial refund processed
- Shipping fees adjusted

### 8.3 Bundle Orders
- Must cancel entire bundle
- Cannot cancel individual bundle items
- Bundle pricing applies
- No partial bundle cancellations

## 9. Marketplace Seller Orders

### 9.1 Third-Party Sellers
- Seller's cancellation policy applies
- Contact seller directly
- Platform may assist
- Seller response required within 24 hours

### 9.2 Platform Intervention
If seller doesn't respond:
- Platform cancels order
- Full refund issued
- Seller penalized
- Customer protected

### 9.3 Seller Cancellations
If seller cancels:
- Full refund immediately
- $5 courtesy credit
- Help finding alternative
- Seller rating affected

## 10. Refused Deliveries

### 10.1 Refusing Shipment
To refuse delivery:
- Tell carrier you refuse package
- Don't open package
- Package returns to us
- Full refund processed

### 10.2 Refusal Refunds
- Full refund including shipping
- Processed when package received
- 7-10 business days total
- No restocking fees

### 10.3 Partial Refusal
For split shipments:
- Can refuse individual packages
- Partial refund for refused items
- Keep other items
- Prorated shipping refund
