# Shipping and Delivery Policy

**Document ID:** POL-002
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 2.0

## 1. Shipping Options

### 1.1 Standard Shipping
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $6.99 (Free on orders over $35)
- Tracking provided
- Available for all US addresses

### 1.2 Expedited Shipping
- Delivery time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99
- Tracking provided
- Order by 2 PM EST for same-day processing

### 1.3 Express Shipping
- Delivery time: 1-2 business days
- Cost: $19.99
- Tracking provided
- Order by 12 PM EST for same-day processing

### 1.4 Overnight Shipping
- Delivery time: Next business day
- Cost: $29.99
- Available for orders placed by 12 PM EST
- Not available for all items or locations

## 2. Free Shipping

### 2.1 Minimum Order
Free standard shipping on orders $35 or more (before tax).

### 2.2 Premium Membership
Premium members receive free 2-day shipping on all orders with no minimum.

### 2.3 Exclusions
Free shipping does not apply to:
- Oversized items (furniture, large appliances)
- Hazardous materials
- International orders
- Alaska, Hawaii, and US territories (additional fees apply)

## 3. Processing Time

### 3.1 Standard Processing
- Orders processed within 1-2 business days
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST processed next business day
- Weekend orders processed on Monday

### 3.2 Custom Items
- Made-to-order items: 5-10 business days
- Personalized items: 3-5 business days
- Custom-sized items: 7-14 business days

### 3.3 High-Demand Periods
During peak seasons (holidays, sales events):
- Processing may take 2-4 business days
- Shipping times may be extended
- Customers notified of delays

## 4. Delivery Confirmation

### 4.1 Signature Required
Signature required for:
- Orders over $500
- Electronics and jewelry
- Age-restricted items
- Customer request

### 4.2 No Signature
Standard deliveries left at door if:
- No signature required
- Safe location available
- Customer has authorized "leave at door"

### 4.3 Delivery Attempts
- Carrier makes 3 delivery attempts
- Notice left after each attempt
- Package returned to sender after 3 failed attempts
- Customer responsible for reshipment costs

## 5. Tracking

### 5.1 Tracking Information
- Tracking number emailed within 24 hours of shipment
- Real-time tracking available on website
- SMS tracking updates available (opt-in)

### 5.2 Tracking Issues
If tracking shows no movement for 5+ business days:
- Contact customer support
- Investigation initiated with carrier
- Replacement or refund issued if package lost

## 6. Lost Packages

### 6.1 Package Marked Delivered
If tracking shows "delivered" but customer didn't receive:
- Check with neighbors and household members
- Check all building entrances and mailroom
- Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
- Contact support after 24 hours

### 6.2 Investigation Process
- Customer support files claim with carrier
- Investigation takes 5-7 business days
- Replacement shipped or refund issued after investigation

### 6.3 Stolen Packages
- Police report may be required for high-value items
- Replacement or refund issued after verification
- Recommend delivery to secure location for future orders

## 7. Damaged in Transit

### 7.1 Visible Damage
If package arrives visibly damaged:
- Refuse delivery if possible
- If accepted, note damage on delivery receipt
- Photograph package and contents immediately
- Contact support within 48 hours

### 7.2 Concealed Damage
If damage discovered after opening:
- Photograph damage and packaging
- Keep all packaging materials
- Contact support within 48 hours
- Provide photos and order number

### 7.3 Resolution
For items damaged in transit:
- Full refund including shipping
- Free replacement shipment
- No return required for items under $25

## 8. International Shipping

### 8.1 Available Countries
We ship to 50+ countries. See website for complete list.

### 8.2 Shipping Times
- Canada: 7-14 business days
- Europe: 10-20 business days
- Asia: 15-25 business days
- Australia: 12-20 business days

### 8.3 Customs and Duties
- Customer responsible for all customs fees, duties, and taxes
- Fees vary by country and item value
- Package may be delayed in customs
- We cannot mark items as "gift" or reduce declared value

### 8.4 International Shipping Costs
- Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight
- Typically $25-$75 for standard items
- Expedited international shipping available for select countries

### 8.5 Restricted Items
Some items cannot be shipped internationally:
- Aerosols and pressurized containers
- Batteries (lithium-ion restrictions)
- Liquids over 100ml
- Food and supplements (country-specific)

## 9. Address Issues

### 9.1 Incorrect Address
If customer provides incorrect address:
- Address correction fee: $15
- Rerouting may not be possible after shipment
- Customer may need to refuse delivery and reorder
- Reshipment costs apply

### 9.2 Undeliverable Address
If carrier cannot deliver to address:
- Customer contacted for correct address
- Package returned to sender
- Refund issued minus shipping costs
- Customer may reorder with correct address

### 9.3 Address Validation
- Addresses validated at checkout
- PO Boxes accepted for small items only
- APO/FPO addresses accepted (extended delivery time)

## 10. Order Modifications

### 10.1 Before Shipment
Orders can be modified or cancelled before shipment:
- Contact support immediately
- Changes subject to availability
- No fees for modifications before processing

### 10.2 After Shipment
Once shipped, orders cannot be modified:
- Customer may refuse delivery for full refund
- Or receive and return per return policy
- Address changes may incur $15 fee if possible

## 11. Split Shipments

### 11.1 Multiple Packages
Orders may ship in multiple packages if:
- Items from different warehouses
- Items have different processing times
- Oversized items require separate shipment

### 11.2 Tracking
Each package receives separate tracking number.

### 11.3 Shipping Costs
Single shipping charge applies even if split shipment.

## 12. Marketplace Seller Shipping

### 12.1 Third-Party Fulfillment
Items sold by marketplace sellers:
- Shipping times vary by seller
- Seller's shipping policy applies
- Displayed on product page before purchase

### 12.2 Combined Shipping
Items from multiple sellers cannot be combined:
- Each seller ships separately
- Separate shipping charges apply
- Separate tracking numbers

### 12.3 Seller Performance
Sellers must ship within stated timeframe or face penalties:
- Late shipment: Customer receives $5 credit
- No shipment after 5 days: Automatic cancellation and refund

## 13. Hazardous Materials

### 13.1 Restricted Shipping
Items containing hazardous materials:
- Ground shipping only (no air transport)
- Extended delivery times
- Cannot ship to PO Boxes
- Additional $10 hazmat fee

### 13.2 Examples
- Aerosols (spray paint, air fresheners)
- Nail polish and removers
- Perfumes and colognes
- Cleaning products
- Batteries

## 14. Oversized Items

### 14.1 Freight Shipping
Items over 150 lbs or large dimensions:
- Freight shipping required
- Delivery time: 7-14 business days
- Curbside delivery (not inside home)
- Signature required

### 14.2 White Glove Delivery
Available for additional fee ($99-$199):
- Inside delivery and placement
- Assembly if required
- Packaging removal
- Appointment scheduling required

## 15. Weather and Natural Disasters

### 15.1 Delays
Severe weather may cause delays:
- Customers in affected areas notified
- Delivery estimates updated
- No expedited shipping refunds for weather delays

### 15.2 Hold at Facility
Packages may be held at carrier facility during severe weather for safety.

## 16. Peak Season Shipping

### 16.1 Holiday Deadlines
For guaranteed delivery by December 25:
- Standard shipping: Order by December 15
- Expedited shipping: Order by December 20
- Express shipping: Order by December 22
- Overnight shipping: Order by December 23

### 16.2 No Guarantees
Shipping times are estimates, not guarantees during peak season.
