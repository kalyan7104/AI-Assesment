# Price Match Policy

**Document ID:** POL-006
**Last Updated:** January 2024
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Price Match Guarantee

### 1.1 Our Promise
We'll match lower prices from qualifying competitors on identical items.

### 1.2 Match Timeframe
- Before purchase: Price matched at checkout
- After purchase: Within 14 days of delivery
- Refund difference if lower price found

### 1.3 Eligibility
Price match available for:
- Identical item (same model, color, size)
- In-stock items at competitor
- Items sold and shipped by competitor (not marketplace sellers)
- Competitor's regular or sale price

## 2. Qualifying Competitors

### 2.1 Approved Retailers
We match prices from:
- Major national retailers
- Authorized dealers of the brand
- Retailers with physical US locations
- Established online retailers

### 2.2 Non-Qualifying Competitors
We don't match:
- Marketplace sellers (eBay, Amazon third-party, etc.)
- Auction sites
- Wholesale clubs requiring membership
- International retailers
- Going-out-of-business sales
- Unauthorized dealers

### 2.3 Competitor List
See website for complete list of approved competitors or contact customer service.

## 3. Price Match Requirements

### 3.1 Identical Product
Must be exact match:
- Same manufacturer and model number
- Same size, color, and specifications
- Same warranty and included accessories
- New condition (not refurbished or open box)

### 3.2 Proof Required
Customer must provide:
- Competitor's website URL or ad
- Screenshot showing price and date
- Proof item is in stock
- Competitor's item number or SKU

### 3.3 Price Verification
We verify:
- Item is identical
- Competitor is approved
- Price is current and valid
- Item is in stock at competitor
- No exclusions apply

## 4. Excluded Items

### 4.1 Items Not Eligible
Price match not available for:
- Clearance or closeout items
- Refurbished or open box items
- Bundle deals or package offers
- Items sold by marketplace sellers
- Limited quantity or flash sales
- Pricing errors
- Gift card promotions

### 4.2 Special Circumstances
- Black Friday/Cyber Monday deals (see section 8)
- Doorbusters and limited-time offers
- Member-only pricing
- Employee discounts
- Student or military discounts

## 5. How to Request Price Match

### 5.1 Before Purchase
At checkout:
1. Contact customer service before ordering
2. Provide competitor information
3. Wait for verification (15-30 minutes)
4. Receive adjusted price or promo code
5. Complete purchase with matched price

### 5.2 After Purchase
Within 14 days of delivery:
1. Contact customer service
2. Provide order number
3. Submit competitor information
4. Verification within 24 hours
5. Refund difference to original payment method

### 5.3 Contact Methods
- Phone: 1-800-PRICE-MATCH (1-800-774-2362)
- Live Chat: Available 24/7
- Email: pricematch@ecommerce-example.com
- Include all required documentation

## 6. Price Match Limitations

### 6.1 One Match Per Item
- Only one price match per item per customer
- Cannot combine with other discounts (unless stated)
- Cannot match multiple competitors for same item

### 6.2 Quantity Limits
- Price match on up to 5 units per item
- Bulk orders require special approval
- Commercial purchases excluded

### 6.3 Price Difference Minimum
- Minimum $5 difference required
- Difference must be on item price (not including tax/shipping)
- Percentage-based: minimum 5% difference

## 7. Competitor Pricing Types

### 7.1 Regular Prices
- Everyday prices matched
- Must be current price
- Not expired promotions

### 7.2 Sale Prices
- Competitor's advertised sales matched
- Must be active at time of request
- Proof of sale dates required

### 7.3 Coupon Codes
- Publicly available codes matched
- Must be valid and active
- No unique/targeted codes
- No expired codes

### 7.4 Not Matched
- Rebates or mail-in offers
- Financing offers
- Free gift with purchase
- Loyalty points or rewards
- Bundle savings

## 8. Holiday and Special Events

### 8.1 Black Friday/Cyber Monday
- Limited price matching during peak events
- Only match identical doorbusters
- Verification may take longer
- Some exclusions apply

### 8.2 Prime Day and Competitor Events
- Match competitor-specific event prices
- Must be publicly available
- Membership-only prices excluded
- Limited to approved competitors

### 8.3 Our Own Sales
- Cannot price match our own future sales
- No retroactive adjustments for our sales
- Exception: Within 14-day window (see section 9)

## 9. Post-Purchase Price Adjustments

### 9.1 Our Price Drops
If we lower price within 14 days of your purchase:
- Automatic refund of difference
- Credited to original payment method
- No request needed (for account holders)
- Guest checkout: contact customer service

### 9.2 Competitor Price Drops
If competitor lowers price within 14 days:
- Request price adjustment
- Same verification process
- Refund of difference
- One adjustment per item

### 9.3 Adjustment Timeline
- Request within 14 days of delivery
- Verification within 24-48 hours
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days
- Notification sent when processed

## 10. Online vs In-Store Prices

### 10.1 Competitor's Online Prices
- Match online prices from approved retailers
- Must be available for online purchase
- Shipping costs not included in match
- In-stock verification required

### 10.2 Competitor's In-Store Prices
- Match in-store prices with proof
- Photo of price tag or ad required
- Must be local store (within 50 miles)
- Store must have item in stock

### 10.3 Our In-Store Prices
- Online and in-store prices may differ
- Cannot match our in-store price online
- Visit store for in-store pricing

## 11. Shipping and Tax Considerations

### 11.1 Price Comparison
Price match based on:
- Item price only
- Excludes shipping costs
- Excludes taxes and fees
- Excludes installation or assembly

### 11.2 Free Shipping
- Our free shipping offers still apply
- Competitor's shipping costs not factored
- Price match on item price only

## 12. Marketplace Sellers

### 12.1 Our Marketplace
- Cannot price match our marketplace sellers
- Each seller sets own prices
- Contact seller directly for pricing questions

### 12.2 Competitor Marketplace
- Cannot match competitor's marketplace sellers
- Only match items sold by competitor directly
- "Sold by [Retailer]" required

## 13. International Pricing

### 13.1 US Retailers Only
- Only match US-based retailers
- Prices must be in USD
- International sites excluded
- Regional pricing differences not matched

### 13.2 Currency Conversion
- No currency conversion for price matching
- Must be advertised in USD
- Exchange rates not considered

## 14. Price Match Denials

### 14.1 Reasons for Denial
Price match may be denied if:
- Item not identical
- Competitor not approved
- Price cannot be verified
- Item out of stock at competitor
- Exclusions apply
- Insufficient proof provided

### 14.2 Appeal Process
If denied:
- Request supervisor review
- Provide additional documentation
- Escalation within 24 hours
- Final decision within 48 hours

### 14.3 Alternative Options
If price match denied:
- Return item per return policy
- Purchase from competitor
- Wait for our next sale
- Sign up for price drop alerts

## 15. Price Match Abuse Prevention

### 15.1 Fraud Detection
We monitor for:
- Fake screenshots or ads
- Photoshopped prices
- Expired promotions
- Out-of-stock claims

### 15.2 Account Restrictions
Accounts with fraudulent requests:
- Price match privileges suspended
- Account review
- Possible account termination
- Legal action for fraud

### 15.3 Good Faith Policy
We operate on good faith:
- Honest mistakes forgiven
- Intentional fraud not tolerated
- Verification protects all customers

## 16. Premium Members

### 16.1 Member Benefits
Premium members receive:
- Priority price match verification
- Extended 30-day window (vs 14 days)
- Automatic price drop monitoring
- Dedicated support line

### 16.2 Automatic Monitoring
For premium members:
- We monitor competitor prices
- Automatic refund if lower price found
- Within 30 days of purchase
- No request needed

## 17. Price Errors

### 17.1 Obvious Errors
We reserve right to:
- Decline obvious pricing errors
- Cancel orders with error pricing
- Not match clearly incorrect prices
- Example: $1,000 item listed for $10

### 17.2 Competitor Errors
- Not required to match competitor errors
- Use reasonable judgment
- Customer service makes final call

## 18. Product Categories

### 18.1 Electronics
- Full price match available
- Model numbers must match exactly
- Warranty must be identical
- Accessories must be same

### 18.2 Appliances
- Full price match available
- Installation not included
- Extended warranties separate
- Delivery fees separate

### 18.3 Furniture
- Price match on identical items
- Custom orders excluded
- Assembly fees separate
- Delivery fees separate

### 18.4 Clothing and Accessories
- Price match available
- Same brand, style, size, color
- Designer items may be excluded
- Seasonal items excluded
